Data strategy

No fake model. No pretend certainty.

NIH / NLM

RxNorm

Used to normalize medicine names into standardized concepts where the public API can resolve them.

FDA

openFDA labels

Used to retrieve current public label sections such as boxed warnings, interactions, contraindications, pregnancy, pediatric, and geriatric use.

Explainability

Traceable findings

Every major warning points back to the label section that triggered it, so the user can see the evidence instead of trusting a black box.

Clinical safety boundary

Review assistant. Not a doctor.

Severe symptoms such as trouble breathing, chest pain, fainting, severe allergic reaction, confusion, uncontrolled bleeding, or suspected overdose require urgent medical care. Do not wait for an app.

Drug safety depends on dose, timing, indication, lab results, patient history, local formulation, and clinician judgment. This tool helps organize risk conversations; it does not prescribe or diagnose.
